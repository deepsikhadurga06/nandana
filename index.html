NANDANA

Nandhana means "one who brings happiness and joy"

My friend Nandhana was born on May 15, and she is someone who naturally brings joy to the people around her. She loves pets and has a soft, caring heart, always showing kindness and affection. She deeply admires Rajinikanth, and her whole family shares that love, which makes it even more special—she even has a hairstyle that reminds us of him. We lovingly call her by nicknames like Vodaka and Alien, which suit her fun and unique personality. Though she often skips food, she never forgets to give advice to others. She is beautiful, loving, and caring, with a mix of boldness and occasional confusion that makes her even more real and relatable.
